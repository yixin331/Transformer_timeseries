This graph represents the rate of New trucks sales in Canada during 2020 . It shows that it starts out at around 4.3 billion dollars in January . It reaches a low of 2 billion dollars in April . From then , it jumps to 6 billion dollars in June . It remains fairly steady until September , reaching a high of 6.8 billion dollars . From there it decreases to about 4.6 billion dollars in December .
