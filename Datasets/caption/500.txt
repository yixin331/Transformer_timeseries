This graph depicts the production of eggs in shell in Canada during 2017 . The unit is farm product price index ( FPPI ) . Production starts off at approximately 1363 in January and goes down to the lowest production at 1362 during February . During the months of March and May the production remains unchanged at 1365 . There is a slight increase of production during June , until it remain stable throughout July until September at 1367 . Then , a small drop can be observed , which is followed by a sharp increase , until it peaks during December at 1371 .
