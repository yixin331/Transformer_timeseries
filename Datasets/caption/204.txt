The average retail price for orange juice in Canada is very unstable throughout 2020 . It starts from around 4.35 dollars/litre in January and keeps increasing to almost 4.40 dollars/litre . It reaches the second-lowest point of lower than 4.25 dollars/litre in March and increases drastically to the peak of more than 4.40 dollars/litre in May . Afterwards , the price constantly decreases to around 4.05 dollars/litre in October and bounces back to almost 4.25 dollars/litre in December . .
