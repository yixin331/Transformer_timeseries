This figure shows the Canadian production of wheat flour of 2016 . Production is measured in tonnes . Production is the lowest during the month of January at approximately 19500 . There is a sharp increase for February , reaching 26000 tonnes , after which a drop is experienced during March . The maximum production of wheat flour is taking place in April at above 30500 tonnes . Production is rather low during May . During the following months production fluctuates until a sharp increase in observed for October , reaching nearly 30000 tonnes . Production remains nearly steady throughout November and drops back to 25000 during December .
