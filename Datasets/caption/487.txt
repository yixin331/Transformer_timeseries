This graph depicts the total Canadian softwood and hardwood production of 2016 . The unit used is cubic metres . Production during January lies at approximately 54000 and experiences an increase until reaching the maximum value in March at above 58300 . From then production slowly declines until June , after which a sharp drop can be observed in July at approximately 51000 . From July until November production levels increase again , reaching a value of nearly 58100 . There is a rapid decline of production in December , which marks the lowest production of the year at 47000 cubic metres .
