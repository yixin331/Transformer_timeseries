The graph shows the production of chickens, turkeys, chicks, and poults in Canada in the year of 2017. There is one data entry for each month of the year. Production is measured by the farm product price index. The x-axis shows the months, the y-axis the index ranging from 1190 to 1215. There is a high in May (index of 1215) and a low in February (a bit below 1190).
