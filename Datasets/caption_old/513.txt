The following graph depicts the Canadian production of oats in tonnes during 2018. In January approximately 160000 tonnes were produced, after which a sharp drop can be observed for February, reaching slightly below 100000. Production remain mostly stable throughout March to May, with the lowest production coming up in July at 80000. Production sharply increases during the following months, until it peaks in October at approximately 210000. Following this there is a drop, and production remains almost unchanged during November and December at 140000.
