This figure represents the land price index in Canada in 2018. Overall the price index increases throughout the year. During January a value of 1037 can be observed, after which it rises up to 1038 during February. It falls back to 1037 during March and April. During the following 2 months there is a steady increase, after which the land price index remains unchanged at 1039 throughout June until August. Also, it remains steady during September and October at 1040. Moreover, the land price index stays constant at the peak value of the year during November and December, reaching up to 1041.
