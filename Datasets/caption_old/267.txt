The chart is showing the values about the production of fresh vegetables in Canada during 2016. The values are illustrated using the farm product price index (FPPI). As can be seen from the graph, from January to May the values have been rapidly decreasing. Then, a peak has been recorded during the month of June, but after that the values started to decrease again until October. During the last two months of the year the production significantly jumped up, reaching the maximum value of the year during the month of December.
